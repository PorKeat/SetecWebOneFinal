Our Story

Since opening its doors in 2000, a small yet remarkable burger shop in Cambodia has built a strong reputation for serving up delicious, high-quality burgers, becoming a staple in the local food scene. Situated in a vibrant corner of Phnom Penh or Siem Reap, this unassuming burger joint has grown into a beloved institution, attracting both locals and international visitors alike with its unique fusion of Western comfort food and Cambodian flair.

Our Vision

Our goal is to become Cambodia's top burger delivery site, offering a seamless, convenient experience with high-quality burgers, from classic favorites to unique, locally-inspired creations, delivered straight to your door.
